Choosing the perfect wallet is a personal decision. It's something you carry every day, and the right wallet can make your life easier while the wrong one is a constant annoyance. Here's our complete guide to finding your perfect match.

## Bifold vs. Cardholder vs. Travel Wallet

The first decision is form factor. Each has its strengths:

- **Bifold**: The classic choice. Holds 6-12 cards plus cash. Best for those who carry a full set of cards and prefer back-pocket carry. Our [Classic Leather Bifold](/products/classic-leather-bifold) is our most popular model.

- **Cardholder**: Minimalist and front-pocket friendly. Holds 4-8 cards. Perfect if you've gone mostly digital but still need a physical wallet. The [Slim Cardholder](/products/slim-cardholder) is a customer favorite.

- **Travel Wallet**: Larger format with passport pocket and extra organization. Essential for frequent travelers. Check out our [Travel Wallet](/products/travel-wallet).

## Leather Types: What Matters

Not all leather is created equal. Here's what to look for:

- **Full-Grain Leather**: The highest quality. Uses the entire grain layer with no surface alterations. Develops a beautiful patina over time. Used in our Heritage collection.

- **Top-Grain Leather**: Slightly sanded to remove imperfections. More uniform appearance. Still excellent quality. Used in most of our bifolds.

- **Shell Cordovan**: The king of leathers. Made from a specific membrane beneath the hide. Incredibly durable and develops rolls instead of creases. Our [Heritage Bifold](/products/heritage-leather-bifold) uses genuine Horween Shell Cordovan.

## RFID Protection: Do You Need It?

RFID-blocking wallets protect your contactless cards from digital skimming. While the risk is relatively low, we include RFID blocking in most of our wallets because the technology adds negligible thickness and provides peace of mind.

## Color and Patina

A quality leather wallet ages beautifully. Lighter colors like tan and natural develop the most dramatic patina, showing every adventure. Darker colors like black and dark brown stay more consistent. Our [Vintage Leather Bifold](/products/vintage-leather-bifold) comes pre-distressed for instant character.

## The Right Choice

The best wallet is one you love carrying. It should feel good in your hand, fit comfortably in your pocket, and hold exactly what you need — nothing more, nothing less.
