## 30-Day Returns

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it within 30 days for a full refund. The wallet must be in new, unused condition with all original packaging.

## Free Exchanges

Need a different color or style? Exchanges are always free. We'll ship your replacement as soon as we receive your return.

## How to Return

1. Contact our support team at support@elora.com
2. We'll email you a prepaid return label (US orders only)
3. Package the wallet securely in its original box
4. Drop off at any carrier location
5. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of receipt

## Damaged or Defective Items

If your wallet arrives damaged or has a manufacturing defect, we'll replace it immediately at no cost. Contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the issue.

## Lifetime Guarantee

Beyond our 30-day return policy, every wallet is covered by our lifetime guarantee against manufacturing defects. We'll repair or replace your wallet free of charge for as long as you own it.
