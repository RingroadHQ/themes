## Frequently Asked Questions

### What leather do you use?

We use full-grain Italian leather from Tuscany for most of our wallets, and genuine Horween Shell Cordovan from Chicago for our Heritage collection. All leather is ethically sourced.

### How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is 2-3 business days. Overnight shipping is next business day for orders placed before 2pm EST.

### Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

### What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day hassle-free returns. If you're not satisfied, return your wallet in new condition for a full refund.

### Do you offer a warranty?

Every wallet comes with a lifetime guarantee against manufacturing defects. If anything goes wrong due to our craftsmanship, we'll repair or replace it.

### How do I care for my leather wallet?

Check out our [Leather Wallet Care Guide](/blog/leather-wallet-care-guide) for detailed instructions. In short: condition every 6 months, avoid water immersion, and embrace the patina.

### Is RFID blocking really necessary?

While the risk of RFID skimming is low, we include RFID blocking in most of our wallets because it adds negligible thickness and provides peace of mind. Read more in our [RFID guide](/blog/rfid-blocking-explained).

### Can I monogram my wallet?

Yes! Our [Engraved Monogram Bifold](/products/engraved-monogram-wallet) supports up to 3 initials in your choice of font and stamping style.

### Do you offer gift wrapping?

All wallets come in a premium gift box. We also offer our [Premium Gift Set](/products/premium-wallet-gift-set) which includes a wallet, key case, and leather care kit.

### How can I contact you?

Email us at support@elora.com or visit our [Contact page](/pages/contact). We typically respond within 2 hours during business hours.
