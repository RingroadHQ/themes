## Shipping Rates

| Method | Cost | Delivery Time |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Free over $75 / $5.99 under $75 | 5-7 business days |
| Express | $14.99 | 2-3 business days |
| Overnight | $29.99 | Next business day |

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed before 2pm EST ship the same day.

## Tracking

You'll receive a tracking number via email as soon as your order ships. You can track your package on our website or through the carrier's website.

## International Shipping

We ship to over 40 countries. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight. International orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties — these are the responsibility of the recipient.

## Free Shipping

Free standard shipping applies to all US orders over $75. The discount is automatically applied at checkout.
