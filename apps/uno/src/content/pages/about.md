## Our Story

elora was founded on a simple belief: everyday objects should be beautiful. A wallet is something you touch every day, and it should feel extraordinary.

We source our leather from the finest tanneries in Tuscany and the legendary Horween tannery in Chicago. Our artisans average 15 years of experience in leather craft, and every wallet passes through at least 12 pairs of hands before reaching yours.

## Our Materials

### Full-Grain Italian Leather

Our primary material is full-grain leather from Tuscany. Full-grain means we use the entire hide thickness - no sanding, no correcting, no shortcuts. The natural grain shows through, and each piece is unique.

### Horween Shell Cordovan

For our Heritage collection, we use Shell Cordovan from the Horween Leather Company in Chicago - a tannery operating since 1905. Shell Cordovan comes from a specific membrane beneath the horsehide and is renowned as the finest leather in the world.

## Our Craftsmanship

Every wallet is hand-cut, hand-stitched, and hand-finished. We use traditional saddlery techniques that haven't changed in centuries because they produce the strongest, most beautiful results.

- **Hand-stitching**: Stronger and more repairable than machine stitching
- **Hand-burnished edges**: Each edge is sanded, dyed, and waxed by hand
- **Individual inspection**: Every wallet is inspected before shipping

## Our Promise

Every wallet we sell comes with a lifetime guarantee against manufacturing defects. If anything goes wrong due to our craftsmanship, we'll repair or replace it - no questions asked, no time limit.

This is our commitment to you.
